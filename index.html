Information We Collect

All financial data you enter (accounts, transactions) and your app preferences are stored exclusively on your device. Nothing is transmitted to any server.

Penny does not collect personal identity information, device identifiers, location data, analytics, biometrics, or cookies.

How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework (backed by SQLite) and UserDefaults for preferences.

Penny has no server, no cloud backend, and no external database. Your financial data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

Your data is protected by iOS app sandboxing and your device's built-in encryption capabilities.

How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used solely to provide the App's core functionality:

  • Displaying your accounts, transactions, and balances
  • Organizing transactions by category
  • Customizing your experience based on your preferences

Your data is never used for advertising, user profiling, or analytics of any kind.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not share, sell, rent, or transmit your data to any third party.

Penny contains zero third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools.

Data only leaves your device if you explicitly initiate an export through the App's share sheet functionality.

Data Export

Penny allows you to export your financial data in JSON or CSV format through Settings.

The export is processed entirely on your device and presented via the standard iOS share sheet. You choose where to save or share the exported file.

Exported files are handled by the destination you choose (e.g., Files, email, third-party apps). Their privacy practices apply.

We have no access to your exported files.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your financial data at any time through Settings > Delete All Data. This action is immediate and irreversible.

App preferences (theme, language, currency) persist after data deletion. To reset preferences, uninstall the App.

Uninstalling Penny from your device removes all associated data completely.

Data Security

Your data is protected by multiple layers of security:

  • App sandbox isolation — iOS prevents other apps from accessing Penny's data
  • Encryption at rest — iOS Data Protection encrypts your data using your device passcode
  • No network transmission — your data never travels over any network
  • No credentials stored — Penny does not store passwords, tokens, or authentication credentials

Children's Privacy

Penny is not directed at children under 13 years of age (or under 16 in the European Union).

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no data leaves your device, no children's data is transmitted or stored externally regardless of age.

Your Rights

Penny is available in the EU, Turkey, and the USA. Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no cross-border data transfers. We do not sell your personal information or engage in behavioral advertising.

Regardless of your location, you can exercise your data rights directly within the App:

  • Access — view your data directly in the App
  • Rectify — edit any entry at any time
  • Erase — delete all data via Settings
  • Portability — export your data in JSON or CSV format

This applies to rights under the GDPR (EU), KVKK (Turkey), and CCPA (California, USA). Our legal basis for local data processing is legitimate interest and your consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Your continued use of Penny after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us
